Milk Pudding

I am a big fan of milk pudding because it is delish. I have not tried this recipe but some japan person thought it tasted pretty similar.

Ingredients

  • 300ml of milk
  • 50ml of fresh cream
  • 20ml of condensed milk
  • 40g of sugar
  • 10g of potato starch
  • A few drops of vanilla flavouring
  • 5g of gelatin
  • 2 tablesppons of water (for the gelatin)

Instructions

  1. Soak gelatin in the water
  2. Put everything else in a pot and mix, heat until it becomes kinda thick and a little bubbly
  3. Combine the gelatin and your weird mixture
  4. Pour mixture into container using a strainer, and chill until set
